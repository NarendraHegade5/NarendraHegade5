Narendra N. Hegade

I am a Ph.D candidate in quantum information theory at International Center of Quantum Artificial Intelligence for Science and Technology (QuArtist), Shanghai University. I am advised by Prof. Enrique Solano. I received my masters's degree in applied physics from National Institute of technology, Silchar, India. My research focuses on breaking the abstraction barrier between quantum hardware and software, with the aim of realizing practical applications sooner than otherwise possible.

  • Master Thesis : Digital Quantum Simulation, supervised by Dr. S. Panda.
    • Simulating quantum system is known to be a difficult computational problem. However, we can overcome from this problem by using some controllable quantum system to study another less controllable and more complex quantum system, this is known as quantum simulation. In this thesis, we discuss and demonstrate the digital approach to quantum simulation.
